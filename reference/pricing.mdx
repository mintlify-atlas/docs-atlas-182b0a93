## What counts as an invocation?

An invocation is recorded each time a workflow or event source executes — regardless of whether it completes successfully or returns an error. Both test runs and production runs count toward your monthly total.

## Free tier limits

The Developer plan includes a hard cap of **10,000 invocations per month**. When you reach the limit:

- New workflow executions are paused until the next billing cycle
- Event sources stop emitting events
- You receive an email notification when you approach the cap

Upgrade to a paid plan to restore execution capacity before the billing cycle resets.

## SOC 2 compliance

Pipedream has demonstrated SOC 2 compliance. A SOC 2 Type 2 report is available to Business and Enterprise customers upon request. Email [support@pipedream.com](mailto:support@pipedream.com) to request a copy.
